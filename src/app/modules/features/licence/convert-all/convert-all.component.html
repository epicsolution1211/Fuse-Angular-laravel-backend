Conversion Permament Licences to The New Multi-Server Licence
All permanent licences will be converted into the one multi-server licence.
The longest maintenance time will be assigned to the newly converted licence.
If you have unused maintenance plans, apply them before converting.
All CCU limits will be added together to the new licence with new limits as below:

Atavism On-Premises Standard 1000 CCU

Atavism On-Premises Advanced 1500 CCU

Atavism On-Premises Professional 5000 CCU

Atavism On-Premises Ultra 15000 CCU
